# GameHub.io

### Course:
CS3300 Course Project 
### Team Members:
Azam, Brandon, David, Trey

## Description:
    GameHub.io is a centralized adless gaming platform, which allows users to play their
favorite games, track their scores, and be ranked against their friends and peers. Many current websites don’t include nostalgic games from our childhood like Hangman and Snake or have features for users to easily track their progress and compare their performance against their friends. GameHub.io solves all of that by creating a centralized website with everyone’s favorite nostalgic games, and friendly competition amongst friends, with an ad-free experience.

## Features
    GameHub features four games built from the ground up, each featuring a scoring system rewarding progress and successive wins in their favorite games. As you rank highest, your name is featured on the left as the highscore holder of that game! The games are:
    -Snake
    -Hangman
    -Type Sprint
    -Path to Paradise

## Setup and Usage Instructions
When you first launch our website, you'll be greeted on our homepage with four recognizable games that can be launched then and there by clicking on them! While Sign-up is an option, it is completely optional, and only necessary for leaderboard purposes. However, if you are interested, simply click on Sign-Up, enter your own username, and enter your chosen password. Make sure to enter it twice to confirm, and then you have your own account for all four of our games!


## Technologies Used
Our server is built from a combination of Javascript ES2025, HTML, and CSS! We worked on it with the help of apps like Github, VScode, and Firebase, which helped us write and simulate the server structure until we could launch it for real. VSCode also featured incredibly helpful tools such as GitLens and Live Server, extensions that have mananged our integrations with Github and allowed local hosted instances of our website during development.
